How to Get Help with Typewriters

If you need assistance with your typewriter, there are several ways to get support:

  1. Contact our customer support team via phone or email.
  2. Visit our physical store for in-person assistance.
  3. Refer to the user manual provided with your typewriter.
  4. Check our online knowledge base or FAQs for troubleshooting tips.

For immediate assistance, you can reach our customer support team at 1-800-TYP-WRTR or email us at support@typewriterheaven.com.

About Typewriter Manufacturer

Remington & Sons (then known for manufacturing sewing machines) who began production on March 1, 1873 under the name Sholes and Glidden Type-Writer. Their model eventually became the Remington Typewriter and it is this machine that popularized the QWERTY layout we are still using on our computer keyboards.. It has contributed significantly to the evolution and advancement of typewriter technology.

Our company, known for its commitment to innovation and quality, has played a pivotal role in the development and popularization of typewriters across the globe. Over the years, we've introduced several groundbreaking models that have revolutionized the way people write and work.

As a leader in the typewriter industry, we've constantly strived to provide durable, efficient, and user-friendly typewriters, catering to the diverse needs of writers, professionals, and businesses.

Our dedication to craftsmanship and excellence has earned us a reputation for producing reliable and high-performance typewriters that stand the test of time.
